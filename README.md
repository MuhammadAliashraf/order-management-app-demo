# OrderMesh: Your Complete Order Management Command Center

> **Streamline your sales, track fulfillment progress, and automate logistics with a single, high-performance dashboard.**

---

## 🌟 Overview

Running a business shouldn't mean drowning in spreadsheets. **OrderMesh** was built for modern entrepreneurs who need speed, clarity, and control. It solves the critical problem of fragmented order tracking by centralizing your entire fulfillment pipeline into a real-time, high-performance interface.

From receiving the first order to marking it as "Paid" and "Delivered," OrderMesh ensures that no detail is lost in the noise.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Live Real-Time Sync**: Powered by a robust cloud backend, your data is always up-to-date across all devices—no refreshing required.
- **Advanced Logistics Integration**: Export specialized order data formatted perfectly for **Postex** and other courier platforms with one click.
- **Intelligent Revenue Tracking**: Instantly view your total revenue from paid orders and monitor your business health via the live stats dashboard.
- **Dynamic Workflow Management**: Transition orders through custom-mapped states: *Received* → *Packing* → *Ready to Ship* → *Delivered* → *Paid*.
- **Bulk Productivity Tools**: Update statuses for dozens of orders at once or export specific groups to Excel for manual analysis.
- **Smart Search & Filtering**: Find any order by customer name, product, or ID in milliseconds using our optimized indexing engine.
- **Sleek, Responsive UI**: A premium dark-mode interface (with light-mode support) designed for maximum focus and minimal eye strain.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

OrderMesh is engineered for speed, stability, and scale:

- **Frontend**: [React 18](https://reactjs.org/) + [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/) for an ultra-fast, snappy user experience.
- **Database / Backend**: [Firebase Firestore](https://firebase.google.com/products/firestore) for instant cloud synchronization and enterprise-grade security.
- **Data Engine**: [SheetJS](https://sheetjs.com/) for complex Excel/CSV exports and data processing.
- **Styling**: Modern CSS3 with a custom-built design system—optimized for performance and aesthetics.
